Types of Seasons

The rainy season is not a fixed season in the Indian Subcontinent with scanty to the heavy rainfall experienced throughout the country.

The months of December, January and February is the span for winter in India.On some days of winter, the sky is cloudy, but on most days it is clear.

Spring is a season of flowers which arrives after winter and before the summer.During the spring season, weather becomes pleasant and the days grow longer.
