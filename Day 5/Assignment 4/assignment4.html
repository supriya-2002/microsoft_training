India Gate

India Gate is a war memorial situated in the capital city of New Delhi, India

It was built to commemorate the 80,000 British Indian Amry soldiers martyred during the first world war

The foundation stone was laid for India gate on 10th February 1921

India gate was officially inaugurated in 1931

Amar Jawan Jyoti was inaugurated by the then prime minter of India, Indira Gandhi

Amar Jawan Jyoti was built to commemorate the soldiers martyred during the 1971 Bangladesh Liberation war
